Privacy Policy

Stack Tower — Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Stack Tower ("the app") handles your information. The app is operated by Bart Geenen ("we", "us").

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect or store any personal information. The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your high score and game progress
  • Purchased themes and coins
  • App settings and preferences

This data may also be synced to your iCloud account if you are signed in, so it is available across your devices.

Advertising

The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads, including a device advertising identifier. You will be asked for permission to track before any personalized ads are shown (on iOS 14.5 and later). You can change your preference at any time in your device Settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Center

The app uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Your Game Center profile and score may be visible to other players on the leaderboard. Game Center data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@stacktower.io
