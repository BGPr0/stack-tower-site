Frequently Asked Questions

My coins or purchases disappeared

Your coins and purchases are stored on your device and synced via iCloud. Make sure you are signed in to iCloud on your device. You can also restore your purchases via the Restore Purchases button in the Shop.

Ads are not loading

Ads require an internet connection and may occasionally not load due to limited availability. This does not affect gameplay. You can remove ads permanently via the Shop.

The game is not responding

Try closing the app fully and reopening it. If the issue persists, restarting your device usually resolves it.
